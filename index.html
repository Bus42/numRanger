Sum all numbers in a range

Want to find every leap year? How about every election year? Start with the last one, enter an end year, and choose 4 for the nth step. How about which years you'll be celebrating your dog's birthday? Maybe you could even calculate the year the world will end! There are lots of other ways to use this simple app.

Choose the starting and ending numbers for your range and, optionally, choose how you would like the program to 'step': I.E. 1 for every number, 2 for every other number, and so on.

The result will be an array of numbers followed by the total of the range.

Example: [1, 4, 7] = 12
